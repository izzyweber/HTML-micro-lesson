Coach utilization model

Global assumptions

Working day is 9–5 with a protected lunch hour near the 12–2 window — lunch shifts by 30 min where that frees a full hour, since tripartites are 1-hour meetings and never split. Tripartites land in the last full working week of each month, replacing that week's sessions (except a cohort's first week). Delivery/prep/meetings recur weekly; tripartite/admin/CPD/other are monthly totals — so 5-week months (June, August) show slightly lower utilization.

Non-programme work per month: alumni support, short courses, business development, and other organisational commitments. Admin and marking is captured separately as it scales with learner numbers.

Working days per week

Capacity scales with working days — e.g. a coach at 2 days/week has 2/5 of full-time capacity. Calendar day placement is unchanged until exact working days are confirmed.

Days off & cover

Select sessions a coach will miss (Ctrl/Cmd-click for multiple, "None" to clear). The whole day is marked off; the session and its prep move to the covering coach.
